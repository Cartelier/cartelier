Welcome to Hi-Scores

Here you will find recommendations for various music videogame soundtracks, according to the owner's personal tastes. The recommendations are sorted by game title. Game franchises are also grouped into one if the gameplay is similar enough that one is indistinguishable from the other

Each game has its own page, with a general overview of each game's gameplay style, aesthetic, and genre. Accompanying the overviews are a couple of recommendations that suitably encompass said style, along with external links to sites where you can purchase and/or listen to them. Some games may only have one notable song, but it is usually worth the inclusion.

Care shall be exercised in making sure that the main singers and composers are credited for their work. If a game series that you think has great music is not on here, please contact the creator through the information in the contact page.
